Mind Map

This game allows you to move through a map of Carl Jung's
model of the human Psyche. You have 5 areas of the psyche to explore:
Self, Ego, Shadow, Personal Subconcious, and Collective Unconcious.
Navigate North, East, West, and South by entering the char n, e, w, or s.
Enter q to quit.

The Self is encapsulated within the Ego and the Shadow.
The Ego is encapsulated within the Personal Subconcious.
The Shadow is encapsulated within the Collective Unconsious.

To play the game, run the Main.java file. If you choose a direction that leads to 
"nowhere", the options main menu will display again to enter another choice.
